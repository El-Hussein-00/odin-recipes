Fried Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps

Step 1

Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin if you prefer

Step 2

Put flour in a large plastic bag. Season the flour with paprika, salt and pepper to taste

Step 3

Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk then, a few at a time, put them in the bag with the flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well.

Step 4

Place the coated chicken on a cookie sheet or tray, and cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper

Step 5

Fill a large skillet (cast iron is best) about 1/3 to 1/2 full with vegetable oil. Heat until VERY hot.

Step 6

Put in as many chicken pieces as the skillet can hold. Brown the chicken in HOT oil on both sides.
